About Us

Welcome to WKNDR!

WKNDR is a platform that allows you to find cool places and experiences near you. We found that many friends had difficulty finding and completing activities because they could not find experiences they all liked or they suffered from analysis paralysis which means that people overthink the many options available and never make a decision.
